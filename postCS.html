Confirmshaming

The dark pattern you just encountered was Confirmshaming. Confirmshaming is the act of guilting a user into opting into something. The option to decline is worded in such a way as to shame the user into compliance. As you can see in the previous example, usually you are presented with a nice offer of 20% off your next purchase. However, in order to get the 20% off, you have to sign up with your email to be on their mailing list. If you don’t want sign up to be on the mailing list, they try to make you feel bad by saying you don’t like to take advantage of good deals. Whether you sign up or not is your choice. I just want you to be aware that you shouldn’t feel bad if you don’t sign up.
