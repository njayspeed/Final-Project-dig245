Roach Motel

The dark pattern you just encountered was the roach motel. The roach motel is a design that makes it very easy for you to get into a certain situation, but then makes it hard for you to get out of it (e.g. a subscription). As you can see in the previous example, it took two clicks and a call to customer service just to cancel a subscription to the website/ company. Instead of making it easy for people to unsubscribe, companies make it harder, thus making it more likely that users will not unsubscribe. Most people will give up on unsubscribing after more than one click and especially after having to call customer service (it will take a long time). Be on the lookout for companies that do this.
